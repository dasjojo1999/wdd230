Target Audience

Small businesses who want to save money and market their products effectively, who work and live in the city of Johannesburg.

Personas

Loyal Lola is a female in her late-30s who has been a data analyst for a profitable SMB for more than a decade. She's happily married with three kids, lives in the suburbs, enjoys taking an annual family vacation, and likes to cook. Lola has a strong need to start her own little side business, but need help marketing her products about cook books

Alex Thomas is a male in his late-20s who has been a financial Advisor, 3 months after college. He is single and finds this the right time to start a small business, that will allow him to be financial free in his forties. Alex is price conscious and responds well to sales and discounts.

scenarios

How did the small business owners work together?

Through meetings with the Chamber of Commerce Professionals Marketing Group, that will help with marketing their businesses, listening to opinions and ideas. Thus making a strategic plan of action to improve marketing.

How will the search for customers be like?

The main driver for marketing will be social media platforms to the target audience.

Why would the small business owners accept a partnership with Chamber of Commerce Professionals ?

Because the Chamber of Commerce Professionals have experience with increasing sales, for small businesses via marketing.

What resources will be made available to members to improve their business?

Courses and lectures done with specialized team.
